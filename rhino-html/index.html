About Our Company

Since its creation ITS Global Construction has emerged as a prefered vendor in the field of Architectural, Civil Engineering, Structural Design, steel Construction, Engineering Construction and Supervision. Our broad capability enables us to offer you full range of quality engineering services, from Design, Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Installing and commission, to Maintenance Management.
